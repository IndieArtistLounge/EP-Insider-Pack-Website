"What Is A Welcome Gate™?"

A Welcome Gate™ is a cross between a popup and a squeeze page. But it's far less annoying and aggressive that both (while still delivering serious results).

Here's how a Welcome Gate works ...

1. When someone goes to your home page, they're redirected to a squeeze page that looks like this, this, or this (the page is at a url lke http://YourSite.com/WelcomeGate/).

2. Your Welcome Gate has a "skip this step" link, so folks aren't required to opt-in.

3. Folks are only redirected to a welcome gate when they go to your home page (i.e. if someone links to a specific blog post on your site - from twitter of their blog - your Welcome Gate won't display).

4. If someone goes to http://www.yoursite.com, they'll be redirected to your Welcome Gate at http://yoursite.com/welcomegate/ ... but if thet get confused and type in your home page URL, they'll simply be taken to your home page.

5. A visitor will only see your Welcome Gate once in their lifetime (unless they go to your site again with another computer or browser, or clear their cookies ... the chances of this is very high).
